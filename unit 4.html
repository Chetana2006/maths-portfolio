Unit 4: Random Variables and Probability Distribution

Random Variables

A random variable is a numerical value assigned to the outcome of a random experiment.

  • Discrete Random Variable
  • Continuous Random Variable

Probability Distribution

A probability distribution describes how probabilities are distributed over the values of a random variable.

Types of Distributions

  • Binomial Distribution
  • Poisson Distribution
  • Normal Distribution

Probability distributions are widely used in quality control, traffic analysis, and reliability engineering.
